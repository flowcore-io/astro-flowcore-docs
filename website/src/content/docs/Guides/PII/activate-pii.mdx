1. In the Flowcore Console, navigate to the tenant you want to activate PII for and click on the **Settings** tab.
<Image src={TenantSettingsImage} alt="Tenant Settings page showing Features section" />

2. Scroll down to the **Features** section and locate **Personal Identifiable Information (PII)**.
<Image src={TenantEnablePIIImage} alt="Enable PII toggle in tenant settings" />

3. Toggle the switch to **On** to enable PII for your tenant.

4. Click **Save Changes** at the bottom of the page to apply your settings.

Once PII is enabled, you can mark event types or activate PII on entities using the Flowcore SDK.

:::note
  PII is disabled by default, so you need to enable it in the tenant settings before you can use it.
:::
